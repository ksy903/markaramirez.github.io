At 28 thieving, you are able to loot 2 chests in East Ardougne containing 3 coins and a Nature Rune:

  1. One is located up the ladder in the cooking range house south of the north bank
  2. Another is located upstairs in the house just east of the gem stall (It is the north most chest in the room).
