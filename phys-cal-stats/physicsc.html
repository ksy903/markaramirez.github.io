Dynamics

Constant Acceleration

$$v_f = v_i + at$$ $$Δx = {v_i + v_f}/2t$$ $$Δx = v_it + 1/2at^2$$ $$Δx = v_ft - 1/2at^2$$ $$v_f^2 = 2a(Δx) + v_i^2$$
